A letter

From me, to you

Hey Val,

I built this because I'm not great with words out loud — but I needed you to know something, and I wanted it to last longer than a conversation.

I know things haven't been easy lately. Your job, your neighbours, the noise of everyday life — and on top of all of that, me. Me being absent, me cancelling, me not always showing up the way you deserve. I see it. I really do. And I'm sorry, genuinely.

What I want you to know is this: the fact that you're still here, still patient, still you — that says more about who you are than anything I could write. You carry a lot, and you carry it with a grace that I notice every single day, even when I'm bad at showing it.

We've known each other for three years. And somewhere along the way — February 14th, to be exact — something clicked. Something I didn't see coming but I'm so glad happened. You were already one of the most important people in my life before that night. Now? I don't have the right word for it yet. But it's real, and it's mine, and it's you.

This isn't just words. This is me, building a small corner of the internet and filling it with you — because that's the only thing I wanted to put in it.

You're not a backup plan. You're not an afterthought. You're the whole thought.

— Always, me
